About this Heritage Gem

The Marigold Palace is more than a hotel; it's a journey into the heart of India's royal past. Nestled in the foothills of the Aravalli Range, this 18th-century heritage estate has been meticulously restored to offer a blend of historical grandeur and modern comfort. Experience the philosophy of "Atithi Devo Bhava" as our staff caters to your every whim with radical warmth and professional precision.
